In this map I applied previously learned concepts, only this time I learned how to edit geometries and their attributes using QGIS. We used QGIS to edit the data because the file, from the King County Data Hub, exceeded the capabilities of GeoJSON to edit data. The legend shows heavily populated areas in dark brown and less densely populated areas in light brown. The units are person per square kilometer. The density levels that were not significant (less than 1000) were assigned the color gray.
