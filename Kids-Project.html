Hey Kids! On this website you can learn some fun facts about the counties of our beautiful State of Washington and their boundaries. Below the map are three buttons: WA Counties, Seattle, and King County's Attractions. Each button displays different fun and iconic places. Start by clicking "WA Counties." Use King County as your starting point. It is highlighted in red, and the rest of the counties are in gray. How many more counties can you name? You can hover the cursor over a county to find out its name. By clicking the "Seattle" button, the boundaries of this great city will be highlighted in the color red. You can hover the cursor over the red area to see a message. By clicking the "King County's Attractions" button you can zoom in to find three fun attractions located in that county. Hint: all three attractions are highlighted in the color green. Two of them are in the northern part of Seattle, and the third is in the Southeast area of Bellevue. You can hover the cursor over each attraction to see a short description of each one. Hint: To restart the original view, simply refresh the tab.
